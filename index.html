Legend Local backbone Specialist organisations (often national) with deep coordination expertise, apply evidence-based interventions, create a strong impact narrative and often have significant fundraising power. Community anchor Deeply rooted local organisations growing into the full backbone role. Long term commitment to the place and existing relationships are a key strength. Local and regional/national backbone Local anchors with dual reporting to specialist regional or national organisations. Particularly relevant where scaling an approach across several places. Public sector backbone Local authority or combined authority as trusted catalyst. Often where civil society lack the capacity or experience in the short term, but can lead to power imbalances. Partnership model Backbone function distributed across multiple organisations and where no single organisation is in the lead.
